This is a post about easing into adult life.

I graduated college a year late, in May of 2025, but most of my friends moved out at the end of the Spring semester in 2024, so I rented a room near the school while I was still taking classes in person.
After the fall semester of 2024, I only had one virtual class left so I moved home.
I started working as a contractor for [The Land Geek](https://www.thelandgeek.com/) on [GeekPay](geekpay.io), through [Techabo](techabo.com) in January of 2025.
In April of 2025 I moved to a small studio apartment in Baltimore, Maryland - about half an hour from my parents, and an hour and a half from where I went to college.
Then in November of 2025 I moved again to 4-person house with friends, just down the street from my old apartment - actually, it was close enough I just carried my bed frame over.
In early 2026, my two colleagues that had been leading the project at [GeekPay](geekpay.io) left, and so I had to take more ownership of it.

I feel extremely lucky for each step in that story.
